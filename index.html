My Work

02

Video game content and mods

Ever since I was a child, I've been fascinated by the world of video games, as much by the universes they create as by the technical and creative processes that govern them. I entered this world by designing mods for several games (including Minecraft, Skyrim, 7 Days to Die, ARK Survival Evolved and Satisfactory).Today, I'm learning to create entire games using Unreal Engine 5 and Unity, as well as languages such as Java, C++ and C#.

3D assets, materials and textures

I use a combination of software and technologies such as Three, Blender, Substance, Gaea and Unreal Engine to design complex assets and environments, elaborate 3D representations of existing locations, and create explanatory animations and plans.

Audiovisual and broadcasting

I create graphic compositions and video elements used for events and social network accounts. An example is the Twitch channelAnosy0_. I've also done live commentary and broadcast management for a few small video game tournaments.

Community management

I've taken part in several projects as a moderator or administrator of communities ranging from 20 to 1200 people, especially online gaming communities. I also often managed communication around these projects.
